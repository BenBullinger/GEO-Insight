GEO-Insight

Which humanitarian crises are most overlooked? A multi-attribute, multi-stakeholder gap-ranking system.

Core idea. Overlookedness as a joint distribution across donor-preference profiles, with an intra-crisis equity correction — not a scalar gap ratio. Four-cell typology distinguishes consensus-overlooked, contested, and sector-starved crises that existing tools conflate. See proposal for details.
