GEO-Insight

Which humanitarian crises are most overlooked?

When nearly every response is underfunded, a single coverage ratio no longer discriminates between crises. GEO-Insight reframes overlookedness as a joint distribution over four donor-preference profiles, with an intra-crisis equity term and a cross-stakeholder disagreement term. A typed five-level semantic layer makes every score traceable to its source data. Validated against two independent human-curated benchmarks.
